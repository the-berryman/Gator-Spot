About us

Gator Spot was founded by Gavin Berryman, a long time Florida Gators football fan. He grew up less than a mile away from the swamp, so hearing the stadium ignite on Saturdays during the Steve Spurrier days was inspirational. Gavin currently works for the University in the Information Technology department, one of UF's biggest entities. He strives to make the Gator community a better place, as well as share a passion for Gator football.
